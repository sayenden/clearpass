Photo Requirements

A good passport photo should have: neutral facial expression, eyes open and visible, plain light background, proper head size (70-80% of frame), no shadows on face, and meet the specific dimensions for your country. Our AI checks all these requirements automatically.

Most countries now prohibit glasses in passport photos due to glare and reflection issues. We recommend removing glasses for the best chance of acceptance. Check your specific country's requirements in our database.

We have special handling for infant photos. Babies don't need to have their eyes open or maintain a neutral expression. Our AI recognizes infant photos and applies the appropriate relaxed requirements while still ensuring compliance.
